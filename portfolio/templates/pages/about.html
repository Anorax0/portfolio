About Me

My short story

Jakub Hebel

I am an energetic and ambitious person, who likes to take a risk. I am data analyst in aircraft company. I prefer working alone (you know, focus on task completely in quiet env), however, working in a group is comfortable for me and for rest of a group, because I want to develop myself with constructive feedback (who can give it to me better than others?).

Quite interested in anthropology and history, so I read a lot and I like searching for certain facts, but I also like handcrafting and travelling. I enjoy playing the guitar as well as video and board games. And I like travelling (yep, everyone loves it - but I do not have Instagram, so...) and you can see it in every photo here (made by my own).

Most interested in python-language programming. I am self-taught person, reading and watching a lot about entire IT world. I had started with helping other person with university project partially written in python (it is most motivating when I have task where I know how it should looks at the end :) )

And a bit later I was coding first scripts in my work (where it was only my initiative) with some task automatization (with brilliant success!). However, I am not software developer, which I would like to be.

And now somebody can think how it has started? (meh... of course nobody was thinking about it, only me). In shortly, let say it began somewhere between 2008 and 2009 (as I remember) with Jarkendar, textual browser game based on Vallheru engine. When it's author decided to close the game, I was forced to find a new place, so i had started my own (not so cool, lets say). It's name was Isgaroth, and I was developing it for 3 years. Learning a lot of PHP, front-end and SQL. However, it was time of high school and golden age of this kind of game was gone, so I was doing different stuff. Now I am on my own way, going to became web developer once again (and yes, it is my little dream to make that kind of game once more, some time :) )
